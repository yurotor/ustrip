§ 05 · QUICK REFERENCE Things to Know.

Subway

Tap contactless card or phone on the OMNY reader.

$2.90 per ride, capped at $34/week per card.

One card per person.

Tipping

Restaurants: 18–20% on pre-tax total. Not optional.

Taxis & Uber: 15%.

Housekeeping: $3–5/day cash on pillow.

Matchday Rules

Clear bag policy strict. Bring nothing or only a small clear plastic bag.

Penn Station ONLY for NYC fans to MetLife.

Mobile ticket at boarding gate.

JFK ↔ Hotel

Yellow taxi flat $70 from JFK + ~$7 tolls + 20% tip = ~$95

No surge. No app needed. Official taxi line.

Return: Uber works fine.

Weather

Late June: 27–32°C, humid. Possible afternoon thunderstorms.

Light clothes, walking shoes, rain layer, hat, sunscreen.

Money & Cards

Cards accepted everywhere. ~$200 cash for tips.

Call your card company to flag US travel.

US billing forms: use Israeli home address.

Emergency

911 · Police, fire, ambulance.

Israeli Consulate NYC: +1-212-499-5000

Lost passport: contact consulate immediately.
