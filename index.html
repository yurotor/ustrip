§ 06 · QUICK REFERENCE Things to Know.

Subway

Tap your contactless credit card or phone (Apple Pay / Google Pay) on the OMNY reader at any turnstile.

$2.90 per ride, automatically capped at $34/week per card.

One card per person — don't share.

Tipping

Restaurants: 18–20% on pre-tax total. Not optional.

Taxis & Uber: 15%.

Hotel housekeeping: $3–5/day in cash on the pillow.

Bellhop: $2/bag.

Matchday Rules

Clear bag policy strict. Bring nothing or only a small clear plastic bag.

Penn Station ONLY for NYC fans to MetLife.

Show NJ Transit mobile ticket at boarding gate. Both legs are pre-purchased.

JFK ↔ Hotel

Yellow taxi flat rate $70 from JFK + ~$7 tolls + 20% tip = ~$95

No surge. No app needed. Just go to the official taxi line outside the terminal.

For return trip, Uber works fine.

Weather

Late June: 27–32°C, humid. Occasional afternoon thunderstorms (intense but brief).

Pack light clothes, comfortable walking shoes, light rain layer, hat, sunscreen.

AC breaks 1–4 PM are smart.

Money & Cards

Credit cards accepted everywhere. Bring ~$200 cash for tips and small things.

Call your card company to flag US travel June 18–28.

For US billing forms, use your Israeli home address (matches the card).

Emergency

911 · Police, fire, ambulance.

Israeli Consulate NYC: 800 2nd Ave, +1-212-499-5000

Lost passport: Contact consulate immediately, allow extra time before flying home.
